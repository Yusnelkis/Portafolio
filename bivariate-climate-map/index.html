Emissions & climate transition
in Europe

Bivariate choropleth: GHG per capita (2023) × emission change (2013–2023), NUTS2 regions (sub-national statistical areas)

How to read this map

Each region’s color tells you two things:

The color — are emissions going up or down?

█ Teal → emissions are falling

█ Magenta → emissions are rising

█ Mauve → little change

The shade — how much does it emit today?

Lighter = low emissions per person

Darker = high emissions per person

Example: dark teal = a big emitter, but improving.

Hover any region for exact figures.
